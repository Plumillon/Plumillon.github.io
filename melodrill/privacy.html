Privacy Policy for Melodrill

Thank you for choosing Melodrill. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Melodrill does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) unless you choose to provide it voluntarily. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Audio Input: Melodrill accesses your device’s microphone to analyze the audio input for tuning purposes. We do not record, store, or share any audio data from your device. The audio input is used solely in real-time for tuning and is not stored or transmitted.
  • App Usage Data: To improve the performance and functionality of our app, we may collect anonymous usage data such as app usage frequency, error reports, and performance metrics. This data does not contain any PII and is used for analytical purposes only.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To provide accurate tuning services using real-time audio input.
  • To improve the app’s functionality, performance, and user experience through usage analytics.
  • To identify and fix bugs and technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

Melodrill may use third-party services (such as analytics tools) to help us understand app usage and improve the app's performance. These third-party services may collect information in compliance with their privacy policies. We ensure that these services do not collect any PII from Melodrill users.

4. Data Security

We prioritize your privacy and employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Audio data is processed in real-time and is not recorded, stored, or shared. We also take measures to ensure that any anonymous usage data is stored securely.

5. Children’s Privacy

Melodrill is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect PII from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this section, and the effective date will be updated. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
